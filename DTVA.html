Welcome to DrewTIM vLogistics

Our Story

DrewTIM vLogistics was founded on January 30, 2020, by sitting CEO/President Andrew Serrazina. The company is comprised of two divisions currently, a trucking division and an aviation division, with the aviation division in a testing stage and the trucking stage fully functional and ready for truckers to join. We are looking forward to a bright future, combining both the truck sim and flight sim worlds with a new piece of software we are in the process of developing. Thanks for your interest, and we hope to you see you on the road or in the skies!
